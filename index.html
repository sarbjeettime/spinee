About the Company

Spine Engineering Limited (formerly Sparrow Trading Ltd.) is a premium construction company engaged in civil construction, mining and electrical works in Sierra Leone.

We provide rental and on-site services for compressed air equipment, dewatering, pumping, earth-moving, concrete and masonry tools, generators, HVAC, lighting, material handling and job-site utility vehicles.

With access to heavy machinery and an experienced full-time team, we deliver land leveling, grading and filling for solar, industrial, transmission line, residential and agricultural projects.

Capabilities

  • Construction, Mining, Rural Electrification
  • Transmission Lines & O&M engineering
  • Excavation, Haulage, Plant feeding
  • Land clearing & development
